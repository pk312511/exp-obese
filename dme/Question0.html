Quick instructions

The option 1 - immediate payment - means that you could take your money away immediately having no risks. You will then be redirected to your next question.

The option 2 - delayed payment - means that you will have the certain amount of money as a later payment -- this payment is always higher than you original plan.

The option 3 - probable payment - means that you will go for a lottery that has an observable possibility to raise your payoff -- but when you lose, you lose all things.

Please alert that all of your answers are individual and they are not correlated with each other; the order of your trial does not matter as well.
